Kickstands Up Support

Need a hand?

For help inside KSU, open Settings → Support & report a bug. For website, account, privacy, or ride-day questions, email info@ridesku.com.

Roadside emergency?
If anyone is in immediate danger, call local emergency services. KSU is not an emergency dispatch or roadside-assistance provider.

Before you send a report

Include what happened, when it happened, your device type, and the app version shown in Settings. Do not send passwords, recovery codes, payment details, or another rider’s precise location unless KSU specifically asks through a secure support flow.

Account and privacy

To permanently remove your KSU account and associated data, use Settings → Delete my account or visit the account-deletion page if you no longer have the app.
