Kickstands Up · Account deletion

Delete your KSU account.

You can permanently delete your KSU account and associated personal data. This is separate from canceling a Google Play or Apple App Store subscription.

In the app

Open Settings → Delete my account, type DELETE, then confirm. This permanently deletes your profile, preferences, bikes, and personal participation data. Solo rides are deleted. Shared rides are canceled, stripped of your identity, and retained for up to 14 days so other riders can see the cancellation.

Without the app

Email info@rideksu.com with the subject “KSU account deletion request” from the email address associated with your KSU account. Do not send passwords, one-time codes, payment details, or government ID. We will use the request to verify ownership before completing deletion.

What deletion does not do

Deleting KSU does not cancel an Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. Cancel any subscription with the applicable store first. KSU may retain limited information for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or financial records, as described in the Privacy Policy.
