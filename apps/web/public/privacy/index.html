Privacy built for riders, not surveillance.

KSU helps riders find, plan, and share rides. We collect only what is needed to provide those features. We do not sell personal information or turn riders into a public live-location feed.

The short version: Joining a ride does not automatically share live location. Exact ride and contact details stay inside the audience you authorize. You can delete your account in the app or request deletion on the web.

Information we collect

  • Account and profile: sign-in identity, email, rider name, profile image, and riding preferences.
  • Ride and community: rides you create or join, invitations, RSVPs, friend connections, messages, and information you add to a ride.
  • Location: approximate or precise device location only when you grant permission for nearby rides, staging-area context, or an explicitly enabled ride feature. KSU does not enable background location tracking in this release.
  • Photos and support material: rider avatars, ride images, and optional support screenshots you choose to upload.
  • Device and service data: app version, platform, diagnostic information, notification token, and limited operational events needed for reliability and security.

How and when we use or share it

We use data to authenticate riders, show authorized rides and approximate ride areas, coordinate invitations and RSVPs, send requested notifications, prevent abuse, answer support requests, and improve reliability. We share information only as needed for features you choose: coordinators and accepted riders can see ride information, while friends see only contact, availability, or ride context you explicitly make available. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

Service providers and retention

KSU uses hosted authentication, database, storage, and notification providers to operate the service. They process information only to provide KSU’s services. We keep data only as long as needed to operate KSU, meet legal or security obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Account deletion and your choices

From KSU, open Settings → Delete my account. You can also request deletion without reinstalling the app at rideksu.com/delete-account. Account deletion removes your profile, preferences, bikes, and personal participation data; limited data may remain where needed for fraud prevention, security, legal compliance, or financial records. You can also manage location and notification permissions in your device settings.

Children and updates

KSU is not directed to children. Do not use KSU if you are under the minimum age required in your location. If this policy changes materially, we will update its effective date and provide notice where appropriate.
